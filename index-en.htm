The Industrial Production Index of Andalusia (IPIAN) is intended to measure the supply of goods produced monthly by Andalusian industry, indicating the monthly evolution experienced by the part of Gross Value Added (GVA) originated from the industrial sector.

  • Organization: Institute of Statistics and Cartography of Andalusia.
  • Scope: Andalusia.

Note:For the adjusted series of seasonal and calendar effects, the Institute of Statistics and Cartography of Andalusia has carried out a methodological modeling approach in accordance with the guidelines of the Statistical Office of the European Union (Eurostat). In this way, when its significance has been verified, the necessary outliers have been introduced in order to model the effect that COVID-19 is having on the series.

As data from subsequent periods become available, it will be analyzed whether these outliers have to be modified by other types of intervention according to the behavior of the different series.
